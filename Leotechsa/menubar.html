About Us




We started in the year 2018 to innovate new areas in the field of robotics and social innovation. With the strong belief that success is the first goal, we have started working on autonomous robotics and social innovation projects. Now, at this stage we have a team of multi skilled engineers having core competency in machine vision; mobile robotics, mechatronics and embedded system design and development.

At Leotechsa, we emphasis on the concept of ideation and conceptualization. We follow the engineering processes and discipline. Our core competency is social innovation and we mainly focus in the complete ecosystem to enrich this goal. We also run skill development program to empower the society with unique experience and learning.



Our Vision

To be a leader in social innovation, providing quality services in robotics and relevant areas of research. Collaboration with leading industries and universities to ensure a continuous learning and innovation, and to achieve excellence in basic-to-advanced skill development training program in the area of robotics.

 

Our Mission

Our engineers are committed and passionate for continuous research and development in the field of social innovation and robotics. This results into successful designing and developing robust, cost effective robotics technology and solutions for the defense industry, academia, private sector and governments all around the world.

We strongly emphasis on continuous skill improvement for our employees and promote the concept of innovative ideation and conceptualization.



Bridging technology to the nature! Beyond the product or project based company, we work on new notions! We combine the elements of fundamental study of physics, mathematics, technology and even psychology to create a whole new art experience for our client and customer. Our ambition is to work on a new line of ideas that focus on aspects such as playfulness, human values and towards nature which we call as social innovation, we also believe in the extended innovation and so we believe in the collaboration, we motivate our team to work with passion.
